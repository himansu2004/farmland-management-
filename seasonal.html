Seasonal Farmers

Seasonal farmers are those who engage in agricultural activities only during specific times of the year, typically aligned with particular growing seasons or crop cycles. These farmers may cultivate crops or engage in other agricultural tasks such as planting, harvesting, or tending to livestock during the peak seasons for their chosen commodities. Often found in regions with distinct climate patterns, seasonal farmers adapt their practices to capitalize on favorable conditions for crop growth and productivity. While their activities may be limited to certain times of the year, seasonal farmers play a crucial role in meeting seasonal demand for agricultural products and contributing to local economies.
